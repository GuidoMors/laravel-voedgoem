Players take turns looking at cards with 5 secret words on it. The other players in their team have 31 seconds to guess the 5 secret words through hints given by them. For each correctly guessed word their team gets a point. The first team to 31 points wins.

Rule 1: You are not allowed to say any of the secret words, or words inside the word, before it has been guessed.
Example: If the word Netherlands is on the card, you are not allowed to say the words Netherlands, nether or land until the word Netherlands is guessed.

Rule 2: You are not allowed to refer to the individual letters of any secret words.
Example: You are not allowed to say that the word starts with an A, or has two P's in it.

Rule 3: You are not allowed to refer to the sound of the word.
Example: You are not allowed to say that the word sounds like japple or grapple.

Rule 4: You are not allowed to say a synonym for the word in another language.
Example: You are not allowed to say that the word is called appel in Dutch.

Rule 5: You are not allowed to use the shorthand or abbreviations of the secret words, nor are you allowed to use the words of the abbreviation if your secret word is an abbreviation.
Example: You are not allowed to say broadcasting if your word is BBC, and you are not allowed to say BBC if your word is broadcasting.

Good hints for the word "Apple": Green round fruit. Brand of the iPhone. Joey's favorite fruit. Fruit I ate this morning. Fruit that Eve stole. Fruit right there on the table. Fruit often used in pies.
